Grounds for divorce under the Hindu Marriage Act, 1955:

1. Adultery
2. Cruelty
3. Desertion for a continuous period of two years or more
4. Conversion to another religion
5. Mental disorder of such a kind that the spouse cannot reasonably be expected to live with
6. Virulent venereal disease
7. Renunciation of the world
8. Presumption of death of the spouse
