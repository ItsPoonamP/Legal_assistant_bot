# Corporate & Financial Laws – Detailed Overview

## Companies Act, 2013

### Objective
Regulates incorporation, management, governance, and winding up of companies in India.

### Key Terms
- **Company**: A body corporate incorporated under this Act.
- **Memorandum of Association (MOA)**: Defines company’s scope and objectives.
- **Articles of Association (AOA)**: Internal rules and regulations.
- **Director**: Individual appointed to manage company affairs.
- **Share Capital**: Capital raised through issue of shares.

### Core Provisions
- Incorporation and types of companies
- Duties and liabilities of directors
- Meetings, accounts, audit
- Oppression, mismanagement, and winding up

---

## Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016 (IBC)

### Objective
Time-bound resolution of insolvency of corporate persons and individuals.

### Key Terms
- **Corporate Debtor**: Entity owing a debt.
- **Financial Creditor / Operational Creditor**
- **Insolvency Resolution Process (CIRP)**
- **Resolution Professional (RP)**

### Core Features
- Moratorium during CIRP
- Committee of Creditors (CoC)
- Liquidation if resolution fails

---

## Competition Act, 2002

### Objective
Prevents anti-competitive practices and promotes fair competition.

### Key Terms
- **Anti-competitive Agreement**
- **Abuse of Dominant Position**
- **Combination** (merger, acquisition)

### Authority
- **Competition Commission of India (CCI)**

---

## SEBI Act, 1992

### Objective
Protects investor interests and regulates securities markets.

### Key Terms
- **Securities**
- **Stock Exchange**
- **Intermediaries** (brokers, merchant bankers)

### Powers
- Registration, regulation, investigation, penalties

---

## FEMA, 1999

### Objective
Regulates foreign exchange transactions and external trade.

### Key Terms
- **Authorized Person**
- **Capital Account Transaction**
- **Current Account Transaction**

---

## MSME Development Act, 2006

### Objective
Promotes development and competitiveness of MSMEs.

### Key Terms
- **Micro, Small, Medium Enterprises**
- **Udyam Registration**

### Provisions
- Classification of enterprises
- Delayed payment protection
